Balloon Analogue Risk Task (BART) is a computer game to measure risk taking behavior of players. It is used by researchers and employers to determine, among others, risk propensity. While there are multiple implementations of BART, there is a lack of implementations where researchers can record data other than just number of pumps or clicks.

Using Django, I was able to record multiple forms of data more than clicks. I further developed this into active inference which helped me analyze player behaviour with better quantifyability. Read more about BART here.
